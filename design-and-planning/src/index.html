About us

Based in London, Havant Park is led by Joe Pettersson, an active member of the European technology and venture capital communities. In addition to Havant Park Joe is VP of Engineering at QuantumBlack, a cofounder of WeTeachDigital and a published author of three books on scaling engineering teams and products. Prior to QuantumBlack Joe was an Associate Partner at McKinsey & Company, advising fast growth tech companies on maturing and scaling their capabilities to meet their growth challenges.
